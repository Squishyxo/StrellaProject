Logo

The Balenciaga brand uses text as its main logo. In many cases we switch it up by using interesting and exciting variants throughout various medium. Our logo is our brand. It speaks for us and it shows the viewers who we are and what we do. Some silhouettes require a different form of brand recognition, therefor we apply our brand logo differently according to the design.
